# Sankalpam - Category Listings

## Service Categories

### 1. **Marriage Ceremonies** 
**For**: Weddings, engagements, pre-wedding functions

**Includes**:
- Bridal wear accessories
- Groom attire items
- Marriage pooja samagiri
- Decorative items
- Ceremonial supplies
- Traditional jewelry accessories

**Typical Package Range**: Basic to Premium

---

### 2. **Seemandham** 
**For**: Pregnancy blessing ceremonies (typically 5-7 months of pregnancy)

**Includes**:
- Traditional seemandham saree
- Jewelry and accessories
- Pooja materials
- Gifts for the mother-to-be
- Decorative elements

**Typical Duration**: Half-day to full-day event

---

### 3. **Naming Ceremony (Naamakaran)** 
**For**: Baby naming ceremonies (typically 12 days after birth)

**Includes**:
- Sacred pooja materials
- Baby gifts
- Decorative items
- Ceremonial accessories
- Invitation support

**Typical Attendees**: 20-100 people

---

### 4. **Temple Functions** 
**For**: Temple pujas, festivals, religious events

**Includes**:
- Temple decoration supplies
- Religious items
- Ritual materials
- Lighting and ambiance items
- Offerings and prasad items

**Customization**: Based on festival/occasion

---

### 5. **House Warming (Griha Pravesh)** 
**For**: New home inauguration ceremonies

**Includes**:
- Auspicious items
- Decorative elements
- Pooja samagiri
- Entry arch materials
- Blessing ceremony supplies

**Typical Guest Count**: 20-200 people

---

### 6. **Wedding Ceremonies (Complete Packages)** 
**For**: Multi-day wedding celebrations

**Includes**:
- Pre-wedding supplies
- Wedding day items
- Reception items
- Post-wedding supplies
- Photography backdrop materials

**Duration**: 2-7 days

---

### 7. **Office-Related Functions** 
**For**: Corporate events, office ceremonies, annual celebrations

**Includes**:
- Auspicious function supplies
- Decorative items
- Ceremonial materials
- Bulk quantities available
- Corporate customization

**Scale**: 20-500+ people
