# Sankalpam - Who We Are

## About Sankalpam

Sankalpam is a trusted and dedicated service provider specializing in **pooja samagiri** (sacred ritual supplies) and comprehensive ceremonial solutions. With deep roots in traditional values and modern service excellence, we have been serving families, temples, and organizations for years.
