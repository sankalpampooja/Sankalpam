# Sankalpam - Business Service Description

## About Sankalpam

Sankalpam is a dedicated service provider specializing in pooja samagiri (ritual supplies) and ceremonial requirements. We cater to individuals, families, temples, and organizations seeking authentic, high-quality religious items and support services.
